## 7 Key Insights (NovaMart / AdPulse Media)

1. **Q4 drives 28% of annual revenue**
   Seasonality index peaks December (1.20x baseline).
   Recommendation: front-load sales hiring in Q3, double inventory/inventory of ad inventory in Sep.

2. **Enterprise team 3x more productive per rep than SMB**
   Same headcount, 3x output per seller.
   Recommendation: reallocate OKR targets — raise Enterprise quota, reduce SMB headcount.

3. **Win rate at 34% — Negotiation → Closed Won is biggest drop**
   55% of deals stall at Negotiation stage.
   Recommendation: introduce deal desk review at Negotiation entry, coach on objection handling.

4. **Video Ads highest margin product at 42% GP**
   vs Display Ads at 28% GP.
   Recommendation: incentivize sellers to push Video in Q3/Q4 upsell motion.

5. **Gaming + BFSI = 52% revenue from only 15% of clients**
   Concentration risk AND growth signal.
   Recommendation: protect top-10 accounts with dedicated CSM. Expand Gaming + BFSI prospecting.

6. **MoM growth stalls June–August both years**
   Summer slump consistent across 2023 and 2024.
   Recommendation: launch retention campaigns Jun–Aug, offer performance guarantees to retain budgets.

7. **Publisher payout averaging 65% of revenue**
   Every 1pp improvement = ~₹X lakh annualized.
   Recommendation: renegotiate publisher contracts for Tier 2/3 inventory. Target: reduce to 62%.
